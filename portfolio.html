Reflections Series

My intention for this series is to display a set of pieces that portray distorted or mutated reflections of different figures. The reflections illustrate how society and other external factors can, in so many ways, change the way people see themselves. Such influences are powerful enough to build, mold, or destroy one's identity. This series calls attention to this issue, and I hope it spreads awareness and warns people of the consequences of their judgements, rumors, and social media rampages.
